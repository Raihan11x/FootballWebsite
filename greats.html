Greats


Lionel Messi is an Argentine professional footballer who plays as a forward and captains both Spanish club Barcelona and the Argentina national team. He is considered by many as the GOAT, he has scored 697 goals in his career and won 6 Balon D'or trophies, which is the most won by any player.

Cristiano Ronaldo is a Portuguese professional footballer who plays as a forward for Serie A club Juventus and captains the Portugal national team. He has scored 725 goals in his career and won 5 Balon D'or trophies.
